OUR PHILOSOPHY

As a small, family-run smart irrigation business, we're dedicated to helping your garden thrive efficiently, keep your lawn lush and your water usage smart. Our cutting-edge technology ensures precise watering, conserving resources while keeping your green spaces thriving. From personalised consultations to seamless installations, we're committed to transforming your garden into a family oasis. 

ABOUT US

Googong Irrigation & Landscape is a local, family-owned and operated business specialising in smart irritation solutions for Googong and surrounds including Jerrabomberra and Queanbeyan (Ngunnawal and Ngambri country).

OUR SERVICES

  • Smart (computer) controlled irrigation systems
  • Irrigation system advice and repairs
  • New Irrigation systems
  • Upgrading existing Irrigation systems
  • New home consultations
  • Smart garden lighting solutions
  • Project management services
  • On-site soil testing (NPK, pH, EC reporting)
  • Plant care regimes

Family-friendly and reliable service.
